Media of the Week

Overview

Duration: one week, January 2023

Solo project

Tools: Swift, XCode, Procreate

Why?

My friends and I love sharing our current media favorites, so I wanted to make an app to help consolidate the information

Challenges

This was my first time building an iMessage extension, so I spent most of the time understanding how they're built. After updating my Mac and XCode, I had some bumps in the road trying to get the simulator to work with my previous XCode version code. I'm still trying to figure out how to get the recieved message to have the same format as the sent message (the square image, not the small oval)

Future

It would be cool to incorporate some kind of data storage so a user can refer to their past favorites!
