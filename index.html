Projects

Journalingo

One of my favorite ways to practice language is through answering randomized prompts/journaling. Journalingo is a space for just that. It utilizes the LanguageTool API, which supports over 30 languages, to proofread journal entries.

Tools: Swift, XCode, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Firestore, LanguageTool API, Procreate

A Playlist for Anything

Making random, very niche or nonsensical (on the surface level) playlists is my favorite hobby. My friends and I would make each other random playlists. This app gameify's playlist making!

Tools: ReactJS, Node.js, Express, Socket.IO, Spotify API

Media of the Week

I love to consume media, and I love to hear about the media my friends consumed. Media of the week is an iMessage extension that allows users to send their media of the week.

Tools: Swift, XCode, Procreate
